Visa-free entry

The citizens of most of the Post-Soviet states, Republic of South Africa, the most of the countries of South America, Israel, South Korea and more - check your policy for possible updates and restrictions

Electronic Visa

E-visa can be obtained by the citizens of 53 countries, including most countries of the Europian Union. Visit the official website (the list of countries unordered there, check the whole list)

Bonuses

It is online, fast, easy, and free of charge. Invitations, hotel booking confirmations or any other documents to confirm the purpose of a trip to Russia is not required. It is not necessary to submit specific travel dates or vehicle information neither.

Characteristics and conditions

You and your accompanying person can apply online for a single-entry visa, and it lets you stay in Saint-Petersburg for up to 8 days starting from the date of entry (for example June 30 - July 7, June 29 - July 6). An applicant should fill in the application form (carefully!) on the website not earlier than 20 days and no later than 4 days before the intended date of entry. Applicants receive a confirmation email within 4 days. A passport must be valid for at least 6 months from the date of applying an e-visa.
